# Research Plan: HRSA and SAMHSA Grant Review Processes and Documentation Requirements

## Objectives
- Detail the grant review frameworks for both HRSA and SAMHSA, including processes, scoring, and criteria.
- Outline the specific documentation requirements for grant applications for both agencies.
- Describe the evaluation standards, including bias mitigation, fairness, and transparency.

## Research Breakdown
- **Area 1: HRSA Grant Process**
  - Sub-task 1.1: Find official HRSA grant review process documentation.
  - Sub-task 1.2: Locate HRSA scoring rubrics and evaluation criteria.
  - Sub-task 1.3: Identify documentation requirements for HRSA grant applications.
  - Sub-task 1.4: Research HRSA's 2025 guidelines and any recent updates.
- **Area 2: SAMHSA Grant Process**
  - Sub-task 2.1: Find official SAMHSA grant review process documentation.
  - Sub-task 2.2: Locate SAMHSA scoring rubrics and evaluation criteria.
  - Sub-task 2.3: Identify documentation requirements for SAMHSA grant applications.
  - Sub-task 2.4: Research SAMHSA's 2025 guidelines and any recent updates.
- **Area 3: General Federal Grant Standards**
  - Sub-task 3.1: Research bias mitigation in federal grant reviews.
  - Sub-task 3.2: Investigate fairness, equity, and transparency standards.
  - Sub-task 3.3: Find comment writing guidelines and reference formats.

## Key Questions
1. What are the key differences and similarities between HRSA and SAMHSA grant review processes?
2. What are the specific scoring components and their weights for typical grants from each agency?
3. Where can I find examples of reviewer worksheets or templates?
4. What are the most recent (2025) policy changes affecting these grant processes?

## Resource Strategy
- **Primary data sources:** Prioritize official government websites, specifically `HRSA.gov` and `SAMHSA.gov`. Also, search for documents on `grants.gov`.
- **Search strategies:** Use a combination of broad and specific search queries such as:
    - `HRSA grant review process`
    - `SAMHSA grant scoring rubric 2025`
    - `HRSA NOFO template`
    - `SAMHSA grant reviewer handbook`
    - `federal grant review bias mitigation`
    - `comment writing guidelines for federal grants`

## Verification Plan
- **Source requirements:** Aim for at least 3 credible sources for each key finding, prioritizing official government publications.
- **Cross-validation:** Compare information from different official sources to ensure consistency.

## Expected Deliverables
- A comprehensive research report in Markdown format.
- A collection of links to source documents, including PDFs of reviewer handbooks, rubrics, and templates.

## Workflow Selection
- **Primary focus:** Search-Focused Workflow
- **Justification:** The task requires gathering a breadth of information first to build a foundational understanding. This initial data collection phase is critical before in-depth analysis and verification can occur.
